City Statistics

Population: 3,898,747 (2020)

Area: 501.55 sq mi

GDP: $1.227 trillion (2022)

A glamourous city in Southern California, Los Angeles is well-known as the national center for film and television production. The City of Angeles has so many tourist treasures besides its iconic Hollywood sign and studios like Paramount Pictures and Warner Brothers.

It is a paradise for art lovers, with numerous galleries and antique shops to choose from. Music fans will have a great time at the Hollywood Bowl for the world-famous performers.

Los Angeles Fashion Week and Fashion District are great places to discover hidden gems like fabric stores and local fashion shows for fashion enthusiasts and professionals. Keep an eye out for vibrant flea markets and street performances all year long (thanks to California's sunshine!).
